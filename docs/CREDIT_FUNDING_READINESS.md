# Credit & Funding Readiness
Educational and organizational module for business credit and funding prep.
## Functional
- Credit readiness score/checklist
- Funding readiness score/checklist/path options
- Document vault record tracker (manual, no secure upload)
- Dispute workspace organizer only
## Stubbed
- Bureau integrations, lender integrations, bank integrations, identity theft submission, secure upload.
## Safety
No guaranteed credit repair, score increases, vendor approvals, or funding approvals.
